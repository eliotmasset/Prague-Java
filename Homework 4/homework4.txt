What happens if another broker goes down ?

For the zookeeper server to function properly, at least three brokers must be online. Otherwise, an error occur.

What if you have 5 brokers and 2 go down ?

Two brokers down is not an issue as we require a minimum of three brokers.

What if you changed the replcation factor for your topic ?

You may select the bare minimum number of UP servers you require by using the replication factor.
